Product and Quotient Rule Jeopardy

Set Up Teams

Read Directions
  1. Read the question aloud and start a 2-minute timer.
  2. Students solve independently on whiteboards.
  3. Do not let students show whiteboards until the teacher has seen all final answers after 2 minutes.
  4. After 2 minutes, have teams hold/show final answers.
  5. Reveal the answer and briefly discuss reasoning or common mistakes.
  6. Use Mark Correct for every team that had the correct answer.
  7. Use Award Points only for the team earning Jeopardy points.
